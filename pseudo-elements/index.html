How to Prepare a Soup

  1. Heat oil or butter in a large pot.
  2. Sauté chopped onions, garlic, carrots, and celery.
  3. Add your choice of broth (vegetable, chicken, or beef).
  4. Stir in main ingredients like veggies, beans, meat, or noodles.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs (like thyme or bay leaf).
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer.
  7. Cook until all ingredients are tender (15–30 minutes).
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  9. Serve hot, optionally with bread or toppings.
